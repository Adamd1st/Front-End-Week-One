Policies & Procedures at Eagle College

Welcome to Eagle College, where academic excellence and integrity are paramount. We strive to provide a safe, supportive, and inclusive learning environment for all students. To ensure a smooth and enriching educational experience, it's essential to familiarize yourself with our school policies, procedures, code of conduct, and other important guidelines outlined below:

1. Code of Conduct:

At Eagle College, we uphold a Code of Conduct that promotes respect, responsibility, and accountability among all members of our community. Key principles include:

  • Respect for oneself, others, and the learning environment.
  • Integrity in academic pursuits, including honesty in assignments, tests, and examinations.
  • Responsibility for one's actions and contributions to a positive school culture.
  • Inclusivity and acceptance of diversity in all its forms.

2. Academic Policies:

Our academic policies are designed to support student success and ensure fairness in assessment and evaluation. Important academic guidelines include:

  • Attendance: Regular attendance is crucial for academic success. Students are expected to attend all classes punctually unless prevented by illness or other valid reasons.
  • Grading: Assessment criteria and grading scales are communicated clearly for each course. Students are encouraged to seek clarification from instructors if needed.
  • Academic Integrity: Plagiarism, cheating, and other forms of academic dishonesty are strictly prohibited and may result in disciplinary action.

3. Student Support Services:

Eagle College provides a range of support services to help students thrive academically, emotionally, and socially. These services include:

  • Counseling: Confidential counseling services are available to support students with personal, academic, or mental health concerns.
  • Tutoring: Peer tutoring and academic support resources are offered to assist students in achieving their academic goals.
  • Accessibility Services: Accommodations are provided for students with disabilities to ensure equal access to education and facilities.

4. Safety and Security:

The safety and well-being of our students are of utmost importance. We maintain a safe and secure campus environment through:

  • Campus Security: Security personnel are present on campus to ensure the safety of students, staff, and visitors.
  • Emergency Procedures: Emergency evacuation routes and procedures are clearly marked and communicated to all members of the campus community.
  • Reporting Concerns: Students are encouraged to report any safety concerns or incidents to campus authorities promptly.

5. Student Conduct and Disciplinary Procedures:

Eagle College has established procedures for addressing student misconduct and disciplinary matters. These procedures include:

  • Investigation: Allegations of student misconduct are thoroughly investigated to gather relevant information and evidence.
  • Due Process: Students involved in disciplinary proceedings are afforded due process rights, including the opportunity to present their side of the story.
  • Disciplinary Action: Depending on the severity of the misconduct, disciplinary actions may range from warnings and probation to suspension or expulsion.

Conclusion: By adhering to our school policies, procedures, and code of conduct, students contribute to a positive and productive learning community at Eagle College. We encourage all members of our community to familiarize themselves with these guidelines and actively participate in upholding our shared values of integrity, respect, and excellence. Should you have any questions or require further clarification, please don't hesitate to reach out to the school administration or relevant campus authorities. Welcome to Eagle College, where every student has the opportunity to soar to new heights!
